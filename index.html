Jiaben Chen

I am an incoming student at Yale University, majoring in Statistics & Data Science. I graduated from WHU of Wuhan University, studied Mathematics and Statistics. During my undergraduate, I received National Scholarship as Top 0.5% student. I have a strong background in security and privacy related research with programming experience using Python/R. Starting from Jan. 2021, I worked remotely with Prof. Yanjiao Chen at Zhejiang University. I am doing research in the areas of deep learning, computer vision and SLAM, under the supervision of Prof. Laurent Kneip at the Mobile Perception Lab. I also worked as a research intern with Prof. Xia Hu at Rice university. Starting from the summer of 2021, I worked remotely with Prof. Jianbo Shi at University of Pennsylvania. My ongoing research topics are audio-visual sound separation and event-based visual odometry.
